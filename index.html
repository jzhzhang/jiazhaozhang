Jiazhao Zhang

I am a Ph.D. student at Peking University, advised by Prof. He Wang since 2022. Before this, I obtained my M.S. degree and B.Eng. degree at NUDT and Shandong University, respectively. During my master's, I was fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Kai Xu and work closely with Prof. Chenyang Zhu.

I'm interested in indoor scene reconstruction, understanding and interaction. More specifically, I work on building robust and practical systems for domestic robots.
